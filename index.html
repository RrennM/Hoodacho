Hi, I'm Dave.

Living in the Pacific Northwest means we see our fair share of wet, cold weather. Personally, I'm a fan of layers, but at a certain point, I start to feel like the kid from that christmas movie who is so bundled, he can't put his arms down! So, I set out to find a fix for this and what I came up with was the Hoodacho. Hoodacho's are a cozier, more versatile alternative to something like a beanie that you can wear with any of your favorite garments, whether hooded or not, to keep you warmer and dry.

Along with being handmade in Oregon, some highlights of Hoodacho's...

  • Can be worn with garments without hoods like vests, jackets, or even, robes.
  • Can fit in existing hoods for extra coziness.
  • Reversible and made with custom fabrics for limitless style options.
  • Waterproof (optional).
  • Can be worn with other headware (ballcaps or beanies).
